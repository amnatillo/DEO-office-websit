Welcome to DEO Female Office

As the District Education Office Female MZD, our mission is to provide high-quality education and support to all students in our district. We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and equitable learning environment that fosters academic achievement, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our department is responsible for overseeing all aspects of education within our district, from early childhood education to high school graduation. We work closely with schools, teachers, parents, and community members to ensure that all students receive the education and resources they need to succeed.

One of our top priorities is to promote student achievement and academic success. We believe that all students have the potential to excel, and we are committed to providing them with the tools and resources they need to reach their full potential. We work closely with teachers to ensure that they have the support and training they need to provide high-quality instruction to their students.
